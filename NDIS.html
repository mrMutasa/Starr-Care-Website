NDIS TERMINOLOGY

What is the NDIS?

The National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) is a program initiated by the Australian Government, designed to provide financial assistance to individuals under the age of 65 with disabilities. This support aims to cover the expenses associated with necessary care and support services.

Who Is Eligible For NDIS

If you are aged between 9 and 65, a local area coordinator can help you to understand the NDIS, apply and connect with other government and community supports. If you have a child younger than 9, an early childhood partner can provide supports to children before they apply, and let families know if the NDIS is right for their child.
