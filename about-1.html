About Us

Exercising Choice And Control

At STARR CARE, we are dedicated to providing exceptional and personalized medical services that prioritize your well-being above all else. Our commitment to your health, comfort, and peace of mind sets us apart as a leading healthcare provider. Our mission is simple yet profound: to empower lives through comprehensive and compassionate healthcare. We strive to enhance the quality of life for every individual we serve by offering a range of medical services designed to address physical, emotional, and mental well-being.

Vision Mission Approach

Our purpose is to empower people living with disability to live a quality life by giving them the support they need to live life to their full potential and capacity.

Our mission is to provide exceptional service to people living with disability and their families. We are committed to making your wellbeing, needs, and goals the focus of our support.

Partnership Our approach is person-centered, actively listening to and responding to your voice. We work collaboratively with your family/support network to identify your needs and goals, in order to provide support that achieves the best outcomes.

Respect: We treat everyone with courtesy, dignity, and respect while valuing their independence. We understand that every person’s support needs are unique, therefore, we focus our work on getting the outcomes that are important to you.

Empowerment: We engage participants with skills and activities that build confidence, promote lifelong learning, and enable them to actively contribute to their community. Our focus is to give you the support you need to live life to your full potential and capacity.

Integrity: We always act with honesty, accountability, and sincerity.

Quality: We value excellence; hence, we pay attention to detail in our service delivery. We strive to give you a service that exceeds your expectations. Your satisfaction is of utmost importance.

Compassion: Our dedicated staff is caring and compassionate. Your wellbeing is of paramount importance to our service delivery.

What Sets STARR CARE Apart

Exceptional Customer Service:

From the moment patients step into a STARR CARE facility, they experience a high standard of customer service. The staff's courteous, attentive, and compassionate approach creates a welcoming and comfortable environment for patients and their families.

Patient-Centric Approach:

STARR CARE is known for its patient-centric approach, placing a strong emphasis on personalized care and building lasting relationships with patients. The healthcare provider takes the time to understand each patient's unique needs, preferences, and concerns, resulting in tailored treatment plans and a higher quality of care.
