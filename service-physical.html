Daily Living/Personal Care

At STARR CARE, we offer support and assistance in developing daily living skills aimed to promote independence. We offer assistance in a variety of core supports and capacity building supports. We aim to build trust and meaningful relationships with you in order to understand how best to provide additional help, depending on your needs. As part of our core supports we offer assistance with tasks like daily living assistance (bathing, dressing, meal prep, etc.), obtaining consumables (incontinence aids, nutrition products), and engaging in social and community activities. Capacity building supports focus on building personal skills and independence, including support coordination and improved living arrangements. Our mission is to assist you to regain independence in different parts of your life.

Support with your daily living and personal care may include assistance with:

  • Personal hygiene: bathing, showering, oral hygiene, dressing and grooming.
  • Menstrual care, toileting, bladder and bowel management
  • Managing feeding cooking, meal prepping and shopping
  • Medicine dosing, wound management and, assistance with injections
  • Domestic skills such as cleaning, laundry, gardening and pet care
  • Budgeting and planning, including for general shopping or even travelling/holiday planning
