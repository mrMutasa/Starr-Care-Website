Our Services

Our Services

Daily Living/Personal Care

We assist you in developing daily living skills and provide additional help depending on your needs to promote independence.

Specialist Disability Accomodation

Our aim is to enable people with disabilities to live independently by providing purpose-built housing options that cater to their high support needs.

Community Participation/ Access

We believe social interaction and community engagement are essential parts of daily life. STARR CARE encourages and assists you in staying connected.

Community Nursing

We have a dedicated team of highly skilled nurses to provide you with the following services in the comfort of your own home.

Respite Care

We believe respite is a great opportunity for our clients to have a break from their daily routine with the support of highly skilled staff in a safe and supportive accommodation.

Transitioning from hospital to home

Our dedicated team has the knowledge and skills to help make your transition from hospital to home smoother.

Positive behaviour support

This method focuses on employing positive strategies to address complex and challenging behaviours, promoting understanding and meaningful change

Peer Mentoring

Peer mentoring is an essential aspect of disability support, emphasising continuous guidance and the enhancement of individual skills.

Psychosocial Recovery Coaching

Our staff is knowledgeable in psychosocial coaching, and our services support people living with a severe mental health condition to actively work on their personal recovery.

What Sets
STARR CARE Apart

Exceptional Customer Service:

From the moment patients step into a STARR CARE facility, they experience a high standard of customer service. The staff's courteous, attentive, and compassionate approach creates a welcoming and comfortable environment for patients and their families.

Strong Community Engagement:

STARR CARE actively engages with the local community through health education workshops, seminars, and community outreach programs. This involvement demonstrates the healthcare provider's commitment to promoting wellness and building a healthier community.

Patient-Centric Approach:

STARR CARE is known for its patient-centric approach, placing a strong emphasis on personalized care and building lasting relationships with patients. The healthcare provider takes the time to understand each patient's unique needs, preferences, and concerns, resulting in tailored treatment plans and a higher quality of care.
